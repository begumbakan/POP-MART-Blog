## My POP MART Experience

The first POP MART store I went to is in the Netherlands, in Amsterdam. I went there on the 6th of February.

We don't have POP MART in my country, so visiting the store in Amsterdam was a unique and exciting experience for me. I was amazed by the variety of collectible figures and the vibrant atmosphere inside.

I always wanted some POP MART figurines, but since I was inexperienced, I couldn't decide what to buy and ended up choosing things I had seen on social media. I don't regret what I bought—they are awesome—but I do regret not getting some other figurines, especially Pucky. Here are the ones I bought:

Overall, my first POP MART experience was unforgettable. I loved exploring the store, discovering new series, and opening each blindbox with excitement. Even though I didn’t get every figure I hoped for, I’m really happy with my collection and can’t wait to add more in the future.
